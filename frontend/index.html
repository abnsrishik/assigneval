What We Built
Every Feature You Need
⏰
Deadline Enforcement
Each assignment gets a unique link that automatically expires at the set deadline. Late submissions are blocked — no manual tracking needed.
🤖
AI Evaluation
Claude AI reads each PDF against the teacher's rubric and assigns marks with detailed feedback. Consistent, objective, instant.
📝
Typed & Handwritten
Typed PDFs are evaluated automatically. Handwritten or image-heavy submissions are evaluated via Claude Vision and flagged if uncertain.
📊
Excel Results
Color-coded Excel sheet auto-generated with all student scores, feedback, and statistics. Download anytime from the teacher dashboard.
🔍
Student Self-Check
Students enter their roll number to instantly view their marks and AI feedback — no login required, no waiting.
⚠️
Review Flagging
Uncertain evaluations are automatically flagged for teacher review. The system knows what it doesn't know — and tells you.
